# Agentic AI Alignment (Amazon Nova)

## Category Statement
APEX-QC is an **Agentic AI system** where autonomous agents use **Amazon Nova reasoning on Amazon Bedrock** to detect incidents, reason across multiple operational domains, resolve conflicts, execute actions, and learn from outcomes.

## Why It Fits
- Agents reason, not just classify: demand, inventory, logistics, pricing, and risk each produce structured decisions.
- Orchestrated autonomy: a master orchestrator activates and coordinates specialized agents in parallel.
- Real-world complexity: handles multi-constraint tradeoffs (ETA, margin, rider shortage, churn risk) in seconds.
- Safe execution: confidence threshold, impact caps, and override-ready control points.
- Continuous improvement: KPI-linked policy updates close the learning loop.

## 20-Second Judge Pitch
“This is not a chatbot and not a static model. APEX-QC is an agentic control system powered by Amazon Nova reasoning on Bedrock. One orchestrator activates specialized agents, resolves cross-domain conflicts, executes guarded actions in real time, and updates policy from outcomes.”
